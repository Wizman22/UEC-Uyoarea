MISSION/VISION

The church exists to glorify God and reach the unreached for Christ through evangelism and missions, equipping and empowering the saints and social action for the benefit of mankind.

We are a purposeful Bible-based Church united in worship and service to God and humanity, holistically towards the rapture.
